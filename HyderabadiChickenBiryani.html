Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani

Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani is one of the most popular Biryani's in india.It's the cooking method that makes it so special and amazingly delicious.This classic biryani is definitely a must addition to your recipe repertoire. This Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani is the King of Biryani's. The Royal biryani perfect for any special occasation. U know, Whenever you want to turn a beautiful day into a special one.

prep time

15 mins

cook time

45 mins

serving

4 servings

Tags : Biryani Food

Instructions

Ingredients

1kg Chicken with Bone

5cups of Basmathi Rice

1 tbsp Garam Masala Powder

3cm fresh Ginger

8 to 10 garlic,cloves

Chilli Powder

Onion, green chilli

10 Ltrs of Water

tools

Knife

Measuring Cups
